Explainable Reasoning

Making AI decisions easy for humans to understand

Explainable reasoning, also called Explainable AI (XAI), means creating AI systems that humans can understand and trust. As AI is used in important areas like healthcare, finance, and law, it becomes very important to know why the AI made a certain decision. This helps ensure fairness and responsibility.

XAI uses different methods to explain how AI works. For example, it can highlight which data influenced the decision or give simple explanations in human language. The aim is to open the "black box" of complex AI models so people can check, fix, and monitor them while still keeping their performance strong.
