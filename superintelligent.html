Superintelligence

An intelligence that is much smarter than humans

Superintelligence is a type of intelligence, usually created by AI, that is far more powerful than the smartest human minds. It could perform better than humans in many areas such as science, creativity, social understanding, and decision making. This idea is often discussed when thinking about the future development of AI.

A superintelligent system could solve very difficult problems like curing diseases, helping with climate change, or discovering new scientific ideas. However, it could also create risks if it is not properly controlled. Making sure such powerful AI systems follow human values is an important challenge for researchers and society.
