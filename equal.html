Equitable Access

Making sure everyone can benefit from AI

Equitable access means that all people can use and benefit from artificial intelligence, no matter where they live, how much money they have, or their education level. It focuses on preventing a situation where only rich countries or large companies have access to powerful AI tools.

To achieve fairness, we need open research, affordable technology, and systems that are designed for everyone. Governments and organizations should also create policies that prevent unfair digital gaps. Different communities should be included in developing AI so it reflects their needs and values. Without careful effort, AI could increase inequality instead of helping society grow.
