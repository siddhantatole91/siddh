A connection between the human brain and artificial intelligence

In biology, a synapse is the place where brain cells communicate with each other. Today, it is also used as a symbol for the future connection between humans and AI. Exploring the synapse means creating smooth and fast connections that allow humans to work with AI systems in real time and improve our thinking, senses, and creativity.

This idea includes technologies like brain-computer interfaces, smart learning systems, and shared digital environments. In the future, instead of only using keyboards and screens, humans may connect more directly with computers. This could help people solve problems faster, improve memory, and work together with AI more effectively.
