AI SAFETY FRAMEWORK

Alignment & Safety

Making sure AI works according to human values

Alignment research is about teaching AI systems to understand and follow human intentions. As AI becomes more powerful and starts making complex decisions, it is important to ensure that it behaves correctly, is easy to understand, and helps people even in new situations.

AI safety focuses on making systems reliable and responsible. It includes things like learning human values, allowing humans to correct AI, and designing systems that fail safely. Without proper alignment, even very smart AI systems could make harmful mistakes.
