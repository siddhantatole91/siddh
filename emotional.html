Emotional AI

Helping machines understand and respond to human emotions

Emotional AI, also called affective computing, is a field of artificial intelligence that focuses on understanding human emotions. These systems use tools like cameras, microphones, and sensors to study facial expressions, voice tone, and body signals to guess how a person is feeling.

Emotional AI can be used in many areas. For example, mental health chatbots can provide supportive conversations, customer service systems can detect when a user is frustrated, and educational software can respond to how interested a student is. As this technology improves, it also raises important questions about privacy, user permission, and how emotional data should be used responsibly.
